## 5. Your Engagement Velocity Problem

### The 24-48 Hour Window

- Most original tweets get **< 10 engagements in first hour**
- This means they **never enter viral recommendation loops**
- Your high-view tweets (523K) got there through **search/direct traffic**, not recommendations

### What This Means:

Your content isn't getting the **early engagement velocity** needed to trigger algorithmic distribution. The algorithm needs:
- 2-5 likes in first 30 minutes
- At least 1 reply in first hour
- Any retweet in first 2 hours

Without this, tweets fall out of the recommendation window.
